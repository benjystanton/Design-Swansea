Directions to TechHub

TechHub Wales, 11 Wind Street, Swansea, SA1 1DP

TechHub is located half way down Wind Street opposite Nando’s. The entrance is next to Café Nissé, you’ll see some big green gates which are locked in the evening. You’ll need to buzz yourself in. Once inside… take the stairs or the lift to the 2nd floor.

By Car

Westbound

M4 Junction 42 (near Jersey Marine). Follow the A483 (Fabian Way) all the way to the city centre. Join the A4067 into Quay Parade (past Sainsbury’s).

Eastbound

M4 Junction 45 (near Morriston). Follow the A4067 towards the Liberty Stadium, then the A4217 into the city centre. Join the A4067 into Quay Parade (past Sainsbury’s)

Parking

By Train

The closest train station to TechHub is Swansea Train Station (SWA) with routes in via First Great Western & Arriva Cymru and is the mainline link through to London Paddington.

On exiting the station there are taxi ranks situated outside, TechHub is a short (10 min) walk from the station via High Street, continue past the Castle on your left onto wind street, half way down this street, left hand side, directly opposite Nandos is TechHub. The entrance is next to Café Nissé.

Swansea
